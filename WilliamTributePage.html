William Trachsel

Husband, Father, Grandfather, Great-Grandfather, WWII POW Survivor

William and Onalee's 70th anniversary

Who was William Everett Trachsel?

William Everett Trachsel AKA "Bill" was born in November, 1923 in Ohio to Senora and Arthur Trachsel. He was a husband to Onalee Parker for 71 years, a father of 5, a grandfather of 13 and a great-grandfather of 24. He passed away peacefully at the age of 93.

Bill's Life in a Nutshell

  • Moved to Detroit
  • Worked at Henry Ford Hospital after high school
  • Met Onalee Parker
  • Went to WWII as a medic
  • Stationed in Ardennes, France
  • Two weeks later, captured during the Battle of the Bulge
  • For 4 months he lived through starvation, constant marching, and unsanitary conditions
  • Returned home in April of 1945
  • Married Onallee Parker
  • Moved to Redford, MI
  • Had 5 kids
  • Worked for PPG
  • Had 13 grandchildren
  • Retired
  • Bought a retirement home in Florida where Onalee and Bill spent their winters swimming, golfing, and playing BINGO
  • Had 24 great-grand children
  • Celebrated his 70th anniversary with his wife in 2015
  • Passed away peacefully at 93 in 2016 surrounded by loved ones
